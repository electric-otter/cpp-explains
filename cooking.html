Delicious Modern Dish: Simple Garlic Pasta

Welcome to a simple guide for a flavorful, quick, and satisfying meal. This recipe focuses on fresh ingredients and straightforward steps, embodying a **sleek and simple** approach to home cooking [2].

Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti or Linguine
  • 3-4 cloves Garlic, thinly sliced
  • 50ml Olive oil
  • Small bunch Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a **boil** [8, 10]. Add pasta and cook according to package directions until **al dente** [10].
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add sliced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using). Cook slowly until garlic is fragrant and lightly golden, being careful not to **burn** it [10].
  3. Drain the pasta, reserving about 1/2 cup of the pasta water.
  4. Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the garlic and oil. Toss to combine.
  5. Add chopped parsley and a splash of reserved pasta water, if needed, to create a light sauce that coats the pasta. Season with salt and black pepper to **taste** [10].
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your **simple, yet elegant** meal!
